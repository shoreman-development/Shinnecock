YOUTH CLUBHOUSE

The Shinnecock Youth Clubhouse is a safe-space for Shinnecock youth, ages 12-21. It is a place that provides education and sharing amongst many different generations. It is located in the basement of the Shinnecock Nation’s Family Preservation Building and has amenities such as: technology, games, a meditation room and modern decor. The Clubhouse’s programming will enhance and provide support for the social, physical, emotional, mental, spiritual, and financial wellbeing of our youth. We will love, honor and support all of our youth, so that they never feel like they are not enough. We will increase their awareness of who they are as Shinnecock people to boost their confidence. Through our youth’s education we will empower them to grow into healthy, strong-minded, well-versed adults that will want to contribute positively to our community and abandon the thought of using alcohol and/or drugs to cope with life.


Shinnecock youth are at high-risk for using drugs and alcohol at young ages due to the prevalence of drug addiction and alcohol use and a lack of activities available for them in our community. The Shinnecock Youth Clubhouse offers consistent programming based around the pro-social concept and Shinnecock culture. The Shinnecock Youth Clubhouse provides drug/alcohol prevention and mental health education, exercise classes, wellness and recreational activities, skill-building, vocational, financial courses, fundraising activities, volunteering, family and community engagement activities, and more. The Clubhouse is in operation in the evenings and on weekends, which are the hours when alcohol and drug use usually increase.
