HOUSING DEPARTMENT

The Shinnecock Indian Housing Rehabilitation Program can help enrolled Tribal Members of the Shinnecock Indian Nation living in their homes with heating and housing repairs, reconstruction and rehabilitation, weatherization, energy efficient upgrade, mold remediation and handicap accessibility ramp and rails. We are also HEAP and Project Warmth certified and can assist with the application process for these programs.


All applications are available in the Housing Department. Housing assistance applications are processed in the order they were received and then, by those receiving assistance for the first time. Elders may be prioritized when a health or safety risk is present. The Housing Program is an income-based program defined by local HUD guidelines. Housing assistance applicants who have previously applied to the program and received assistance must wait a 2-year period before reapplying. A copy of the guidelines are available in the Housing Office.
