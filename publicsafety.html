PUBLIC SAFETY

To provide security services to the Shinnecock Indian Nation through mobile patrols and stationary post, which will afford for the detection of hazardous conditions and or behavior that may pose a threat to the well-being and safety of community members, as well as any property or assets belonging to the Shinnecock Indian Nation.


To continuously seek to enhance the quality and professionalism of the services provided to the Nation. To be a deterrent to unwanted behavior and or individuals by maintaining an omni presence throughout the Territory. Work with the community to address concerns and activities which may impede our continued quest to enhance the quality of life, as well as the maintenance of our culture, peace and harmony which we have always enjoyed!
