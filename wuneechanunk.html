WUNEECHANUNK SCHOOL

The Wuneechanunk Shinnecock Preschool provides young children with the opportunities to achieve their full, unique potential through comprehensive, culturally sensitive, and early childhood care and education, planned and implemented in partnership with families and the community.


WSP provides child care for children between the ages of 6 weeks and 3 years old and for children turning 4 after December 1st. We split our children up in 3 different classes: Ohke ( 6 weeks to 18months), Nippe ( 18months to 36 months), and Kesuk ( 3years â€“ 4years old). This year due to COVID-19 we assisted working families in our community by opening our Nepauz class for school age children where the family had their children do remote learning. We also helped families by offering outside and family care service options, which is a provision of our CCDF Tribal grant as well.


Wuneechanunk Shinnecock Preschool is opened Monday- Friday 7:30am to 5:30pm.
