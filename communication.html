COMMUNICATION DEPARTMENT

The communication department, known as The Nation's Voice, is committed to keeping all tribal members, both on and off territory, informed with important information and current events. Historically the department focused on monthly newsletters highlighting past and future events. We are currently turning our primary focus to emails and social media. In this digital age, the tribe must get the word out as quickly and efficiently as possible. The Nation's Voice sends out almost weekly emails ranging from tribal updates to essential notices from the local public schools to job opportunities in the community. The Nation's Voice also acts as the unofficial mouthpiece for the Council Of Trustees, using the platform to make all of their official announcements.


Tribal members are also encouraged to submit job opportunities, resources, and information. Our emails and social media posts are intended for enrolled members only. To submit or to be added to the email list, please send an email to nationsvoice@shinnecock.org
