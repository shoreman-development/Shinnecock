The mission of the Shinnecock Nation Tribal Enrollment & Vital Records Office is to protect, honor and adhere to the governance set forth by the Constitution of the Shinnecock Nation and the Enrollment Ordinance. The Enrollment Department strives to ensure that each person and department be treated fairly, compassionately and with total cooperation in regards to membership, potential membership, and other tribal business. Our office practices total confidentiality pertaining to department business.


Tribal Enrollment Services

  • Issue Tribal IDs
  • Issue Verification Letters
  • Issue Hunting Licenses
  • Issue Eagle Feather Applications
  • Verify and Track Citizen Benefits
  • Verify and Track Student Attendance in Local Schools
  • Respond to and Advocate on Indian Child Welfare Cases
  • Track Shinnecock Territory Residents and Residential Data
  • Collect, Analyze and Report Citizenship/Residential Data and Statistics
  • Collect Vital Records, Research Tribal Genealogy and Protect Tribal Data
