ELDERS' PROGRAM

The Shinnecock Elder’s Program intention is to honor all tribal elders with dignity and respect. To offer opportunities for social interaction, cultural enrichment, recreation, wellness education, nutritional meals, exercise and volunteerism. To administer, manage, deliver and sustain a thriving elder program all within a safe and healthy environment. The Shinnecock Elder’s Program understand the value tribal elder possess through their knowledge, wisdom and guidance.



These are important values that keep our cultural traditions vibrant, imparting the ways of past generations to our future generation. Children learn by what they see, elders set a guide of truth and action. Our tribal elders appreciate bonding with the youth through multi- generational social events. Whenever tribal elders come together it is a blessing, one that is not taken for granted. The mutual respect shown, the laughter as they reminisce in enjoyable recollection of past times. Our Shinnecock elders reveal to us looking behind fills you with gratitude, looking forward fills you with vision, looking upwards fills you with strength, looking within you discover peace. Therefore, we honor our tribal elders for they have the wisdom to teach what we have not learned yet.

Karen Torrence
